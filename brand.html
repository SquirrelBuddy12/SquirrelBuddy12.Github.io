Oodles of Yum Bakery

For the sweetness of life

Mission Statement

To provide our Guests with the most incredible tasting cakes, pies, and dessert treats to satisfy the whole range of customers, from children to adults. We will do this with quality ingredients and creative pastry cooks.

Vision Statement

To be the best known bakery and to become a neighborhood place where people will celebrate all their achievements in life or just come for a sweet fix with friends.
